How to connect to the cluster and start jobs?

  1. Connect to the university VPN.
  2. Use the following command to connect via SSH:
    ssh yourcs@{{SLURM_LOGIN_NODE}}

You can find more information in the Wiki at {{WIKI_LINK}} (Login required).

How to get an account?

If you do not yet have an account:
  • If you are a faculty member, you likely have an LDAP account. It is usually your u:account with a "cs" suffix. This account is used to login to the cluster.
    However, you have to be added to the list of "allowed" accounts (i.e., to the slurm database). Please write an email and include the following information, so we can add you:
    • Your CS account name.
    • Your research group(s).
  • If you are a student, please write an email with (at least) the following information:
    • Your u:account name.
    • A description why you need access (e.g., your project, supervisor, ...).
  • If you are a faculty member but do not have an LDAP account, please ask your local technician whether you can get one ... and then either write us your LDAP account or your u:account name.
